# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: March 2026**

## Our Privacy Commitment

Locusify is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our service.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials to provide access to the service.

### Photo Data

- Photos you upload are processed **locally on your device** to extract GPS coordinates and metadata.
- We do **not** upload, store, or retain your original photos on our servers.
- Location data extracted from photos is used solely for map display and trajectory replay.

### Analytics

We use anonymous page-view analytics (Google Analytics) to understand how the app is used and to improve the experience. No personal data is shared with analytics providers.

## How We Use Your Information

- **Account management**: To authenticate you and provide access to your account.
- **Map display**: To render your photo locations on the map.
- **Service improvement**: Anonymous usage data helps us improve the app.

## What We Do NOT Do

- We do **not** collect personal information beyond what is necessary for the service.
- We do **not** sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.
- We do **not** store your photos or location data on our servers.
- We do **not** track your real-time location.

## Data Storage and Security

- All photo processing happens locally on your device.
- Account data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption.
- We use secure HTTPS connections for all communications.

## Your Rights

You may:

- Request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.
- Opt out of analytics tracking through your browser settings.
- Contact us with any privacy-related questions or concerns.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via email.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website or GitHub repository.
